Roasted Garlic Pasta

Garlic Main 30–40 min

This dish is built on mellow roasted garlic—soft, sweet, and savory—then lifted with herbs and a touch of citrus.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta
  • 1 head garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parsley or basil
  • Lemon zest (optional)
Prep: 15 min
Cook: 20 min
Difficulty: Medium

Steps

  1. 01

    Roast the garlic

    Slice the top off the garlic, drizzle with oil, wrap, and roast until soft.

  2. 02

    Build the sauce

    Squeeze the garlic into a pan, mash it, then stir in cream and seasoning.

  3. 03

    Toss and finish

    Toss pasta in the sauce and finish with herbs and lemon zest if desired.
