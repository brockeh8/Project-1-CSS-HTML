Herb Lemon Chicken

Herbs Main 40 min

This dish combines fresh herbs, citrus, and savory depth for a balanced main course that feels bright, grounded, and easy to pair.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp parsley
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • Salt and black pepper
Prep: 15 min
Cook: 25 min
Difficulty: Medium

Steps

  1. 01

    Season the chicken

    Coat the chicken with olive oil, garlic, herbs, lemon zest, salt, and pepper.

  2. 02

    Sear and cook

    Sear the chicken in a hot pan until golden, then continue cooking until fully done.

  3. 03

    Finish with lemon

    Add fresh lemon juice at the end and let the chicken rest briefly before serving.
