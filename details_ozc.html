Orange Zest Cake

Citrus Dessert 55 min

This cake leans into citrus brightness with soft orange aroma, tender crumb, and a clean finish that feels both light and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp orange zest
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
Prep: 20 min
Cook: 35 min
Difficulty: Medium

Steps

  1. 01

    Prepare the batter

    Mix the flour, baking powder, and salt in one bowl while creaming butter and sugar in another.

  2. 02

    Add the citrus

    Beat in the eggs, orange zest, orange juice, and vanilla until smooth and fragrant.

  3. 03

    Bake and cool

    Fold in the dry ingredients, pour into a prepared pan, and bake until golden before cooling fully.
