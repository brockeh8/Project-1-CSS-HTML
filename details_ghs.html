Green Herb Salad

Herbs Appetizer 10 min

This salad is light, fresh, and herb-forward, built to bring brightness and balance to the table with crisp greens, citrus, and soft garden flavors.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1/2 cup parsley
  • 1/4 cup basil
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper
Prep: 10 min
Cook: 0 min
Difficulty: Easy

Steps

  1. 01

    Prep the greens

    Wash and dry the mixed greens, parsley, and basil so the salad stays crisp.

  2. 02

    Slice and combine

    Slice the cucumber and chop the green onions, then combine them with the greens and herbs.

  3. 03

    Dress and toss

    Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper together, then toss with the salad just before serving.
